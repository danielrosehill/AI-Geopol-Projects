The purpose of this repo is to gather together a curated list of projects focused on AI-powered geopolitical wargaming and policy simulation.

This includes projects using LLMs, multi-agent systems, and agentic AI for geopolitical scenario modeling, wargaming, policy simulation, conflict modeling, and strategic forecasting.

When organising the information into README.md, adhere to these preferences:

- Organise by section
- Use shields.io badges for GitHub repos with star count and last commit
- Short descriptions copied from projects
- Author notes in italics to distinguish from project descriptions
- Separate sections for websites and demos/POCs
